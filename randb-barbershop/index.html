Our Story

A corner spot that became part of the routine

R&B Barbershop sits right there on El Camino Real, in the kind of location where you'd drive past a hundred times before you actually stop in. That's usually how it goes — someone tells you, "Hey, there's this barbershop," and you think nothing of it. Then you go once, and suddenly you're one of those people calling ahead to make sure your barber's in.

The barbers here actually talk to you. They listen to what you want, not what they think you should get. Whether you're after a tight fade, a clean-up, or something specific, they know the difference between doing a job and doing it right. That attention is what gets people coming back — it's not flashy, just honest work.

After 75 reviews and a 4.5-star rating, it's clear this place has earned trust the old-fashioned way: by showing up, doing good work, and treating people like they matter. In a neighborhood that moves fast, R&B Barbershop is the kind of steady place you hold onto.

What We Do

Classic barbering with the kind of attention you actually notice.

Haircuts

A real haircut — not rushed, not one-size-fits-all. Your barber will listen to what you want, work with your hair type, and actually deliver what you asked for. You walk out looking the way you meant to.

Fades & Trims

Clean lines, sharp edges, precision work. Whether it's a tight fade, a cleanup on the sides, or a full shape-up, this is where the craft shows. You'll feel the difference in how it looks and how long it holds.

Beard Work

A trim, shape, or full grooming for your beard. They know how to work with different styles and growth patterns, so your beard looks intentional, not accidental. Takes five minutes or thirty — whatever it needs.
