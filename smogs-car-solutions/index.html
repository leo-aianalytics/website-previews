Our Story

The shop that's been keeping Daly City moving

On Sylvan Street, just off the main drag, there's a garage that's become the place neighbors recommend to each other. Smogs Car Solutions didn't get here by cutting corners or overselling repairs. It got here by showing up early, staying late when needed, and telling customers the truth — even when the truth is just "your car's fine."

The team knows that most people don't want to think about their cars. They want to drive them. So when something goes wrong, they walk you through what's happening, why it matters, and what actually needs to happen next. No jargon. No upsell. Just straightforward work and an honest assessment.

Over time, word spreads. A parent tells their kid about this place. Someone from the neighborhood mentions it to a coworker. Now people come in expecting that same honesty and care, and that's exactly what they get. It's become the kind of shop where you don't dread bringing your car in — you trust the person looking at it.
