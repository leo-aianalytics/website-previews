Our Story

A craft that's been trusted for years

Vadim's sits right on The Avenue in San Bruno — the kind of place you'd walk past a hundred times before stepping in, then wonder why you hadn't gone sooner. The shop has built its reputation quietly, one haircut at a time, becoming the spot where neighbors know they're getting the same quality whether it's their first visit or their fiftieth.

What keeps people coming back is simple: Vadim and the team actually listen. They're not rushing through appointments or trying to upsell you on products you don't need. They'll talk you through what works for your hair type, your lifestyle, your face shape — and they're honest if something won't work. That kind of straightforward care is rare.

You can feel it the moment you walk in. There's no pretense here, just skilled hands, good conversation, and the satisfaction of leaving looking and feeling better than when you arrived. That's why people keep coming back, and why their Yelp reviews keep reflecting what regular customers already know.
