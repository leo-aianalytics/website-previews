Our Story

A quiet place that became a neighborhood tradition

Royalty Nails has been quietly doing good work on Newpark Mall Road for years, building a reputation one customer at a time. It's the kind of place where regulars know they'll be greeted by name, where the staff remembers that you always get your thumbs a different color, and where there's never any rushing you out the door.

The technicians here take real pride in their craft — they're not just painting nails, they're listening to your ideas, offering honest suggestions, and making sure every detail is exactly how you want it. That attention shows in the small things: they keep the space clean and calm, they use quality products, and they genuinely seem to enjoy what they do.

People keep coming back because Royalty Nails feels like the opposite of a factory. It's a place where you can relax, chat a little, or just sit quietly with a magazine. That's rare enough that customers drive across the Bay to see the same technician, and that says everything about what this place has built.

What We Offer

Classic and creative nail care, done with care and attention to detail.

Manicures & Pedicures

You'll settle into a comfortable chair, and the technician will take time understanding exactly what you want before the work begins. Whether it's a simple polish refresh or an intricate design, there's no feeling of being rushed through.

Gel & Acrylics

Long-lasting nails that still look natural and feel good. The technicians here know how to shape and finish them so they're beautiful from every angle, not just the front.

Nail Art & Special Designs

If you have a vision — whether it's for a wedding, a night out, or just because — they'll work with you to make it happen. Bring photos, describe what you're imagining, and watch it come to life on your nails.
