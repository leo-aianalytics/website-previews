Our Story

Before dawn on 24th Street

The bakers at Panaderia La Mexicana start before 5 AM. By the time the Mission District wakes up, the shelves are already full — rows of conchas in pink and chocolate, golden cuernos, polvorones dusted with sugar, and trays of empanadas still warm from the oven.

This is not an artisan bakery trying to reinvent bread. This is the real thing — traditional Mexican baking the way it's been done for generations. The recipes don't change. The flavors don't change. And judging by the line out the door on Saturday mornings, neither does the love.

Cash only? Yeah. But nobody complains once they taste the tres leches. 137 Yelp reviews and a 4-star average from a bakery that has never once asked anyone to leave a review. The pan dulce does the talking.

What We Bake

Baked from scratch before dawn. Every. Single. Day.

Pan Dulce & Conchas

The full range — conchas, cuernos, polvorones, orejas, empanadas. Baked in batches throughout the day. Still warm when you grab them.

Tres Leches & Celebration Cakes

Rich, moist, soaked in three milks. Order one for a birthday, a quinceañera, or a Tuesday. We don't judge.

Bolillos & Teleras

Crusty, fresh-baked Mexican rolls. Perfect for tortas, molletes, or just torn apart with butter and coffee.
