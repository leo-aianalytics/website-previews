Our Story

Where International Boulevard tastes like home

Walk into Taqueria El Paisa on a Saturday morning and you'll see what we mean. The place fills up fast — families, construction workers, regulars who've been coming for years — all crowding around the counter for breakfast tacos and fresh-pressed tortillas. This is the kind of taqueria that doesn't need a fancy website or Instagram presence to pack the place. It just needs to be good, and it is.

The people behind the counter move with the kind of efficiency that only comes from doing something right, day after day. They remember orders, they know the regulars by name, and they care about getting it perfect every single time. You can taste it in the carne asada, in the homemade salsa, in the way they prepare everything fresh while you watch. This isn't fast food — it's food made the right way, as fast as quality allows.

Nearly 540 reviews and a 4.4-star rating on Yelp isn't an accident. People keep coming back because Taqueria El Paisa understands something simple: show up, do the work, treat people right. No shortcuts, no gimmicks. Just honest tacos, fresh ingredients, and a space where everyone is welcome. That's why neighbors bring their families here, why regulars stop by multiple times a week, and why you should too.

What you'll find here

Everything a real taqueria should be — breakfast, lunch, dinner, and nothing fancy about it

Breakfast tacos

Start your morning the right way. Carne asada, chorizo, eggs, beans — however you like it, wrapped in fresh tortillas. People line up for these before 10 AM.

Carne asada & al pastor

Meat cooked properly, with the kind of flavor that makes you understand why this is what people order. Watch it come off the grill — that's how you know it's good.

Fresh everything

From the salsa to the guacamole to the tortillas, nothing sits around waiting. Everything is made fresh, which is probably why people taste the difference and keep coming back.
