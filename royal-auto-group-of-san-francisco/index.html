Our Story

A shop that grew with the neighborhood

Royal Auto Group started on South Van Ness back when this stretch was mostly warehouses and body shops. As the Mission Bay area transformed around them, they stayed put, building a reputation one honest repair at a time.

What sets them apart isn't just the work—it's how they talk to you. The team here knows that car trouble is stressful, especially in a city where you need your wheels to get around. They explain what's wrong in plain English, show you the parts, and give you options that fit your budget.

Over the years, they've worked on everything from beat-up Honda Civics to luxury BMWs, treating each car like it matters. That's how you end up with neighbors driving across town just because they trust these guys with their ride.
