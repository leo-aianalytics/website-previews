Our Story

Growing roots on 7th Street

Long before the tech towers sprouted around SOMA, the Hoogasian family was already making this corner of 7th Street bloom. What started as a small neighborhood flower shop has grown into the kind of place where three generations of the same family come for their wedding flowers.

Walk into their shop any morning and you'll find them arranging stems that arrived before dawn from the flower market. The Hoogasians still hand-pick every rose and personally design each arrangement, whether it's a simple 'thinking of you' bouquet or an elaborate corporate installation for the nearby offices.

It's the kind of place where they remember that Mrs. Chen from Potrero Hill prefers white lilies, and they'll stay late on Valentine's Day to help the guy who forgot until 6 PM. After all these years on 7th Street, they've become part of the neighborhood's most important moments.
