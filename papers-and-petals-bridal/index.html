Our Story

From sketch pad to aisle ready

What started as a small alterations shop has become the place Burlingame brides whisper about to their friends. Papers & Petals found its home on Burlingame Avenue when the founder realized that most bridal shops treat alterations like an afterthought — but here, it's the main event.

The team has an eye for the tiny details that make or break a wedding day look. They've worked with every designer label and budget find, understanding that whether your dress cost $500 or $5,000, it needs to fit like it was made just for you.

Brides keep coming back because they remember how it felt to slip into their perfectly altered dress for the first time. That moment when everything just clicks — the hemline hits exactly right, the bodice hugs without pinching, and you finally look like the bride you imagined.
