Our Story

The neighborhood standard for 20+ years

When you've been pressing suits and hemming dresses on Ocean Avenue as long as the team at Ocean Dry Cleaning has, you learn that every garment tells a story. That wedding dress needs to be perfect. That interview suit could change someone's life. That vintage jacket passed down from grandpa? Irreplaceable.

The family behind the counter treats each piece like it belongs to their own closet. They've mastered the delicate dance of removing impossible stains, reshaping wool that seemed beyond hope, and making alterations so seamless you'd swear the garment was made for you originally.

It's the kind of place where regulars don't even need to explain what they want anymore — just a knowing nod and 'the usual.' Where they remember you prefer light starch, and they'll call if they think that stain might not come out completely. In a city that changes fast, Ocean Dry Cleaning stays steady, reliable, and absolutely meticulous.

What we do best

Professional care for everything in your closet

Dry Cleaning

From everyday work clothes to delicate vintage pieces, we handle fabrics that can't take a trip through your washing machine. Each item gets individual attention and the right treatment for its specific needs.

Professional Alterations

Whether you need pants hemmed, a jacket taken in, or a dress completely resized, our alterations make your clothes fit like they were custom-made. We work with everything from basic adjustments to complex tailoring.

Stain Removal

Red wine, ink, mystery marks — we've seen it all and removed most of it. Our stain treatment process is thorough and gentle, often saving garments that other cleaners might write off as hopeless.
