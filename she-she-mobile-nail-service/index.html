Our Story

Nails on your schedule, not ours

She She Mobile Nail Service started with a simple idea: why should you have to rush to a salon during your lunch break or squeeze in an appointment between meetings? Instead of another storefront, they built their business around coming to you — whether that's your home in the Sunset, your office downtown, or even your hotel room if you're visiting the city.

The team brings professional-grade equipment and a full range of polishes and treatments to every appointment. They've figured out how to recreate the relaxing salon experience in your living room, complete with the little touches that make getting your nails done feel like a treat rather than a chore.

What keeps clients calling back isn't just the convenience — it's the personal attention you get when your nail tech isn't juggling three other customers. You get their full focus, your favorite music playing, and no rushing to make room for the next appointment. It's how nail care should be.
