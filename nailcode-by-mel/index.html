Our Story

How a passion for detail became everyone's favorite nail spot

Walk into Nailcode by Mel on Stevens Creek, and you'll notice something right away: it's calm. There's no pressure, no rushing through clients like they're on a conveyor belt. Mel built this place on a simple idea—that getting your nails done should feel like you're spending time with someone who actually cares about the work. Over the years, that approach has turned regulars into friends.

Mel's background is in nail artistry, and you can see it in the details. She doesn't just file and polish; she studies what works for your hands, your lifestyle, what you're going for. Whether it's a clean, no-fuss manicure or something more creative, she brings a technical eye and genuine interest to every appointment. Her clients aren't just satisfied—they're loyal.

The 55 five-star reviews aren't a coincidence. People come back because they trust her, because the prices are fair, and because they know they'll leave feeling like their nails are actually healthy and cared for, not just decorated. In a neighborhood full of generic strip-mall salons, Nailcode by Mel stands out as the place where you're a person, not a transaction.

"I've been coming to Mel for almost two years now and I honestly can't imagine going anywhere else. She remembers exactly how I like my nails done, the conversation is always genuine, and my nails stay perfect way longer than anywhere else I've tried. She's a keeper."

What We Do

Every service is tailored to you, not rushed through a checklist.

Manicures

Mel takes time to prep your nails properly, listens to what you want, and delivers clean, long-lasting color that actually looks intentional. You'll leave with nails that feel as healthy as they look.

Pedicures

A proper pedicure here means attention to cuticles, skin texture, and the kind of detail that keeps your feet feeling pampered for weeks. Mel uses quality products and techniques that don't leave you with razor burn or regret.

Nail Art & Design

If you want something more creative, Mel brings her artistry to the table. She can work with your ideas, suggest designs that suit you, and execute them with the precision that comes from years of practice.
