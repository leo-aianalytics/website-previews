Our Story

The neighborhood's laundry room

Not every laundromat is equal. Sunset Suds keeps its machines clean, its space bright, and its Wi-Fi fast. Located on Irving Street in the Inner Sunset, it's the kind of place where you can knock out your laundry, grab a coffee next door, and get on with your day.

Whether you're doing a quick load before work or dropping off a week's worth of clothes, we make it easy. Coin, card, Apple Pay, Android Pay — however you want to pay, we've got you. And if you'd rather not wait, just leave it with us.

What We Do

Self-service and drop-off laundry, open every day of the week

Self-Service Wash & Dry

Modern, high-efficiency machines in multiple sizes. Bring a book or use our free Wi-Fi — most loads done in under an hour.

Drop-Off Fluff & Fold

Work, browse, or stream while your laundry runs. Clean seating, good lighting, and reliable Wi-Fi — not an afterthought.
