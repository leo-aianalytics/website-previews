Our Story

The shop that became part of the neighborhood

Bayshore Auto sits on Bayshore Boulevard near the heart of Visitación Valley, a place where word-of-mouth still matters more than ads. The shop opened because someone saw a need—reliable, straightforward car repair without the corporate runaround. Over the years, it's become the kind of spot where regulars know to arrive early on a Saturday morning, where the waiting room has real coffee and actual conversation, and where your 2008 Honda gets the same attention as a newer car.

The mechanics here aren't trying to upsell you on work you don't need. They'll tell you straight: what needs fixing now, what can wait another month or two, and what you should keep an eye on. That kind of honesty builds trust, and trust is what keeps people coming back—sometimes for 5 years, sometimes more. You see the same faces behind the counter, the same hands under the hood.

People come to Bayshore Auto because they know they'll get treated like a neighbor, not a transaction. There's a reason the place has nearly 130 reviews and a 4.9-star rating—it's because when your car breaks down on a random Tuesday and you need it fixed before Wednesday, you call someone you actually trust. That's worth more than any fancy waiting room.

"Been taking my car here for five years now. They fixed my transmission and didn't try to convince me I needed a whole new engine like the dealer suggested. Honest people. Fair prices. That's all you need."

What We Do

Real repairs, honest conversations, no surprises when you pick up your keys.

General Auto Repair

Bring your car in with whatever's bothering you—strange noises, warning lights, things that just feel off. The mechanics will spend time actually diagnosing the problem instead of guessing, so you know exactly what needs to happen next.

Tire Service

Whether you need a patch, a replacement, or an alignment to make sure they wear even, the team handles your tires like they're on their own car. You'll get recommendations based on your actual driving, not what makes the biggest margin.

Preventive Maintenance

Oil changes, fluid checks, brake inspections—the stuff that keeps surprises from happening on the freeway at 6 AM. Catch small things before they become expensive problems, on a schedule that makes sense for your car.
