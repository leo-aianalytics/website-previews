Our Story

A neighborhood fixture with a personal touch

Nail Designs by Sunny sits tucked into Serramonte Center, the kind of place that's become part of the rhythm of Daly City life. It's not flashy—just a clean, welcoming space where Sunny and her team have built something that feels less like a transaction and more like a ritual. After years of doing nails here, she knows her regulars by name, remembers what they like, and genuinely gets excited about seeing them walk through the door.

What sets this place apart is the attention to detail—both in the work and in how people are treated. Sunny's not rushing through appointments to fit in more bodies. She listens to what you want, offers real suggestions based on what will actually work for your lifestyle, and takes her time getting it right. Whether it's a simple polish change or intricate nail art, you can tell she takes pride in the work leaving her station.

That's why the reviews speak for themselves. People don't just come back because their nails look good (though they do). They come back because they feel heard, respected, and treated like they matter. In a world that moves too fast, Sunny's created a quiet pocket of Daly City where you can sit down, relax, and walk out feeling a little more like yourself.

What We Do

Everything from simple manicures to custom nail art—done with care and attention

Manicures & Pedicures

Take a seat, pick your color, and let Sunny work her magic. You'll leave with hands and feet that feel cared for and look fresh, whether you're going for clean and classic or something with a little more personality. It's the kind of service that makes you remember why a good mani-pedi matters.

Gel & Acrylics

Want nails that last longer and look flawless? Sunny specializes in gel and acrylic applications that feel natural and hold up to real life—not just Instagram photos. She'll help you choose what works best for your hands and lifestyle.

Nail Art & Custom Designs

If you've got a vision—whether it's intricate patterns, seasonal designs, or something totally custom—Sunny can bring it to life. She loves the creative challenge and takes real satisfaction in seeing someone's face light up when they see the finished result.
