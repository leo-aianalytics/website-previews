Our Story

The tailor who built Campbell's trust

Bo Lee's shop on East Campbell Avenue has that lived-in feeling of a place that's earned its reputation one careful stitch at a time. Tucked into Suite 200, it's the kind of spot locals whisper about when someone needs a wedding dress hemmed perfectly or a suit that actually fits.

Bo brings decades of old-school tailoring skills to everything from last-minute formal wear fixes to complete bridal alterations. The kind of precision that comes from years of understanding how fabric moves, how bodies are shaped, and how the right fit can make someone stand a little taller.

Maybe it's the way Bo remembers returning customers, or how they never rush a job even when you're cutting it close to your event date. Nearly 600 reviews later, people keep coming back because they know their clothes—and their trust—are in the right hands.
