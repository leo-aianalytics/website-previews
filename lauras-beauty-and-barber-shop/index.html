Our Story

The little shop that the Mission calls its own

On the stretch of 24th Street between Bryant and Florida, tucked into a neighborhood that's seen a lot of change, Laura's Beauty & Barber Shop has held its ground. It's not flashy — there's no neon sign or app to book on — but on any given Tuesday afternoon you'll find a full house, regulars swapping stories in the chairs, and the quiet hum of clippers doing their thing.

Laura runs her shop the old way: good work, fair prices, and no rush. She knows what you want before you finish describing it, and she'll tell you honestly if something won't suit your face. That kind of straight talk is rarer than it sounds, and it's a big part of why people who grew up in the Mission still make the trip back when they need a cut.

Over 150 people have left reviews, and almost all of them say some version of the same thing — they felt taken care of. Not pampered in a spa-day way, just looked after by someone who actually pays attention. In a city where everything is always changing, that kind of consistency means something.

"I've been coming here since I lived on 23rd Street, and I still make the drive even though I moved to the Sunset two years ago. Laura just gets it — I barely have to explain what I want anymore. The price is unbelievably fair for San Francisco. Honestly one of those places I never want to see change."

What We Do Here

Clean cuts, careful work, and a price that doesn't make you wince

Haircut

Sit down, say your piece, and let Laura do the rest. Whether you're after a tight fade or a simple trim, she takes her time and gets it right — and you'll leave looking like yourself, only sharper.

Beard Trim & Shaping

A good beard trim is half the battle, and Laura treats it with the same care as the cut itself. She'll clean up the edges, shape the line, and make sure the whole thing looks intentional.

Kids' Cut

Bringing a squirmy kid into a barber chair can feel like a gamble, but Laura's relaxed pace and patient hands make it easy. Parents in the Mission have been trusting her with their kids for years, and it shows.
