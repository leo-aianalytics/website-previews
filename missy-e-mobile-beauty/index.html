Our Story

A one-person show that became the neighborhood's go-to

Missy started doing nails out of frustration. She'd see friends and neighbors stressed about finding time for self-care—driving across town, waiting in line, feeling rushed through their appointment. A few years back, she decided to flip that script: why not bring the salon to them? What began as weekend work in Dublin has grown into something real, with a steady stream of regulars who book her because she actually listens to what they want.

The mobile setup isn't a gimmick—it's intentional. Missy keeps things small and personal. She does nails, lashes, and waxing with the kind of attention that comes from genuinely caring about the work, not churning through appointments. Her hands are steady, her eye for detail shows in every manicure, and she remembers how you like your nails shaped or whether you're sensitive to certain products.

People come back because they feel respected. You're not a ticket number or a 30-minute slot. You're someone who deserves to feel good about how you look, and Missy treats it that way. The 100+ five-star reviews aren't accidents—they're from folks in Dublin who've found something rare: a beauty service that actually feels personal.
