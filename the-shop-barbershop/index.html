Our Story

A corner of 24th Street that always has your back

Tucked between Potrero Ave and Utah Street, The Shop Barbershop is the kind of place that quietly becomes part of your routine. You start coming in for a cut, and somewhere along the way you realize you've been telling your barber things you haven't told anyone else. That's not an accident — it's what a real neighborhood barbershop does.

The craft here is taken seriously. Whether you're getting a clean fade before a job interview or just keeping your edges sharp on a Tuesday morning, the barbers at The Shop know that a good cut is more than scissors and clippers. It's attention. It's listening. It's the difference between someone who cuts hair and someone who actually sees you.

People come back to The Shop because it feels familiar in the best way. Saturday mornings at 7am when you want to beat the rush. A Wednesday afternoon when you've got somewhere to be. The hours are built around real life, and the work speaks for itself. In a neighborhood that's changed a lot over the years, some things stay steady — and this is one of them.

What We Do

Straight-up, skilled barbering — no fluff, just craft

Haircut

A proper cut tailored to your head, your face, your life. Your barber takes the time to understand what you want and delivers something that still looks good three weeks later.

Fade

Tight, clean, and blended just right — the kind of fade that makes you do a double-take in every reflective surface on the walk home. Whether it's a low skin fade or a mid taper, the attention to detail shows.

Beard Trim

Your beard deserves the same care as your cut. The Shop shapes and cleans up your beard so it looks intentional, not just grown — the finishing touch that pulls the whole thing together.
