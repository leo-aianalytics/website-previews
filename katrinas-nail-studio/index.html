Our Story

Katrina's vision: nails done with intention

When Katrina opened her studio on Stanwell Drive, she wasn't thinking about volume or quick turnovers. She was thinking about the woman who'd been coming to the same salon for ten years and never felt heard, or the person who wanted to try something bold but got talked out of it. She wanted to build a place where that changed.

Every technician here actually listens — like, stops and listens. They'll spend an extra five minutes understanding what you really want before picking up a brush. That attention shows in the work. You can tell when someone cares about the small stuff: the shape of your nail, the balance of the color, how it'll look in natural light, not just under studio lights.

It's why people drive across town to come back. It's why the reviews keep stacking up, genuine and specific. You don't build a 5-star reputation by accident. You build it by showing up, day after day, and treating each set of nails like it matters. Because at Katrina's, it does.

What We Create

Every service is tailored to you — not rushed, not one-size-fits-all.

Gel Manicures

You sit down, talk through exactly what you want, and watch it come to life. The color lasts weeks without chipping, and the whole experience feels like someone actually cared about your hands. It's the kind of polish job that makes you look at your nails all day.

Acrylics & Extensions

Whether you're growing them out or starting fresh, the technicians here shape and finish with precision. You'll walk out with nails that feel like they're actually yours — strong, long, and exactly the look you were hoping for.

Pedicures

It's more than a foot soak and polish. You get the leg massage, the attention to detail on every toe, and that feeling of actually being taken care of. Leave feeling like you just had a real break.
