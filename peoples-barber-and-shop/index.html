Our Story

A Barbershop That Actually Belongs Here

Tucked on Townsend just off Colin P Kelly Jr Street, People's Barber & Shop sits in a corner of SoMa that's seen a lot of change over the years — tech offices, new condos, the whole transformation. Through all of it, this place has stayed exactly what it is: a barbershop where people come to get a good cut without any nonsense attached to it.

The barbers here have a way of reading what you actually want, not just what you say. Whether you walk in knowing exactly what you need or you're the type who just says 'same as last time,' they've got you. They open early most days and stay open late, because they know San Francisco doesn't run on a nine-to-five schedule, and neither do they.

What keeps people coming back — and you can see it in the reviews, in the regulars who show up every few weeks like clockwork — is that feeling of being taken care of. Not pampered, not upsold. Just taken care of. In a city that moves fast and doesn't slow down much, having one place that feels genuinely steady is worth more than people say out loud.
