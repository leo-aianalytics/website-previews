Our Story

The nail artist who makes house calls feel personal

Lavinia started doing nails in her friend's kitchen fifteen years ago, word spreading through her tight-knit community about the woman who could make anyone's hands look like they belonged in a magazine. When she finally opened her own space in Visitacion Valley, half the neighborhood showed up to celebrate.

What sets Lavinia apart isn't just her steady hand or eye for color—it's how she remembers that Maria prefers her cuticles barely touched, or how she always asks about Jennifer's new baby while painting the perfect shade of coral. She sources her polishes from small brands and isn't afraid to mix custom colors on the spot.

Her regulars book the same time slot every two weeks, treating their appointments like therapy sessions where they happen to leave with gorgeous nails. It's the kind of place where you feel like family from your first visit, and where Lavinia will text you if she finds the perfect shade for your skin tone.

What We Do

Nail care that's equal parts artistry and self-care ritual

Classic Manicures

The kind of precise, clean manicure your grandmother would approve of—cuticles shaped just right, polish applied with museum-quality attention to detail. Lavinia takes her time because she knows your hands tell your story.

Gel & Shellac

Long-lasting color that actually lasts, applied with the patience of someone who's mastered the craft. She'll walk you through aftercare like she's teaching her own daughter, because two weeks from now, she wants your nails to still look perfect.

Nail Art & Design

From delicate florals to bold geometric patterns, Lavinia treats each nail like a tiny canvas. She sketches ideas on paper first, collaborating with you until the design feels exactly right for your personality and lifestyle.
