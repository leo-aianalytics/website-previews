Our Story

A cut above the corner shop

There's a stretch of Divisadero between McAllister and Fulton that has a certain pulse to it — coffee shops, record stores, the kind of foot traffic that tells you a neighborhood is alive. Fresh Blades found its home right in the middle of it, and it didn't take long for word to get around. People walking by would peek in, see someone leaning back in the chair looking completely at ease, and think — yeah, I'm coming back here.

The barbers here know their craft the way a good cook knows their kitchen — no wasted motion, no showboating, just clean lines and a steady hand. They'll talk to you if you want to talk, give you space if you don't, and they'll remember how you like your fade the next time you walk in. That kind of attention is rarer than it should be.

People keep coming back to Fresh Blades for the same reason they keep going to their favorite spot for coffee or a slice — it just feels right. Over 106 Yelp reviews later, and the thing that comes up again and again isn't just the cuts. It's that feeling of walking out onto Divisadero looking exactly how you wanted to look. That's what this place is about.

"I've tried probably six or seven barbershops since moving to the city and Fresh Blades is the one I keep returning to. The guy who cuts my hair remembers exactly how I like my fade without me having to describe it every single time. That alone is worth five stars."

What We Do

Classic barbering done right — no gimmicks, just good work

Haircut

A proper sit-down where your barber actually listens to what you want before the clippers start. Whether you're going tight on the sides or keeping it longer on top, you'll leave looking clean and intentional.

Fade & Shape Up

This is where the craft really shows — a precise fade blended smooth with a shape up that frames your face the way it's supposed to. It's the kind of detail work you notice every time you pass a mirror.

Beard Trim & Line Up

Your beard deserves the same care as your cut, and here it gets it. A clean line up and a careful trim that works with your face shape, not against it.
