Our Story

The cobbler on Bush Street

Some mornings on Bush Street, you'll catch the rhythmic tap-tap-tap of hammer on leather before you even see the shop. This narrow storefront between Jones and Leavenworth has been the neighborhood's shoe sanctuary for years, where the smell of leather conditioner and rubber cement tells you you're in the right place.

The magic happens in plain sight — no fancy machinery, just skilled hands, quality materials, and the kind of patience that's become rare in our throwaway world. Whether it's resoling a pair of Red Wings that have walked every hill in the city or polishing dress shoes back to their former glory, every repair is treated like a small resurrection.

People don't just come here to fix shoes; they come to save memories. The hiking boots from Patagonia trips, the heels worn to countless first dates, the work boots that built careers — this is where San Francisco keeps its sole.

What We Fix

Bringing your favorite pairs back from the brink

Shoe Repair

From worn-out soles to broken zippers, loose stitching to separated heels — we tackle the problems that would send most shoes to the trash. Every repair comes with the satisfaction of knowing your favorites will walk with you a little longer.

Professional Shoe Shine

More than just a quick buff, this is the full treatment your leather deserves. We clean, condition, and polish until your shoes look like they just stepped out of the store, not off the streets of San Francisco.

Heel & Sole Replacement

When the foundation gives out, we rebuild from the ground up. New rubber soles, fresh heel tips, or complete resoles that'll have you walking on clouds instead of concrete.
