Our Story

A steady chair in a city that never sits still

Right in the middle of Embarcadero Center — where office workers grab coffee, tourists check their maps, and the Bay wind cuts through the plaza — Pro-Style has been quietly doing what it does best: giving people a great haircut and sending them back out into the world looking sharper than when they walked in. It's not flashy. It doesn't need to be.

The barbers here have a way of making you feel like a regular even if it's your first visit. They pay attention. They remember how you like your fade, how short you go on the sides, whether you want to talk or just zone out for twenty minutes. In a neighborhood full of suits and schedules, that kind of personal attention is rarer than you'd think.

Over a hundred Yelp reviews and a solid 4.6 stars — not from a barbershop that's been around for decades, but from one that's earned real loyalty from real people who work and live nearby. Folks come in on their lunch break from the nearby office towers, or squeeze in a visit before catching BART back across the Bay. They come back because the cut is consistently good, the price is fair, and nobody wastes their time.

"I work two blocks away and started coming here maybe a year ago. Consistently one of the best haircuts I get anywhere — and I've been to shops all over the city. Clean, efficient, and the barber remembers exactly how I like my fade every single time. Worth every dollar."
