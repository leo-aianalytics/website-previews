Our Story

A hidden gem on 8th Street

You wouldn't expect to find one of San Francisco's most beloved bridal tailors tucked away in a second-floor suite between Brannan and Townsend, but that's exactly where Paris Connection Bridal has been working their magic for years. What started as a small alteration shop has become the place where Bay Area brides come when they need their dress to fit like it was made just for them.

The team here understands that your wedding dress isn't just fabric and thread — it's the centerpiece of one of the most important days of your life. They've hemmed everything from vintage finds to couture gowns, always with the same careful attention to detail. Whether you bought your dress online and it's three sizes too big, or you found the perfect vintage piece that just needs a little love, they know how to make it work.

What keeps brides coming back (and referring their friends) isn't just the skilled tailoring — it's the way they make you feel. No judgment if you've gained or lost weight since ordering your dress, no rushing through fittings, just honest advice and expert hands that understand how a wedding dress should fit and move. In a city where good tailoring is hard to find, they've built something special.

What We Do

From perfect hems to complete transformations

Wedding Dress Alterations

Whether it's taking in the bodice, hemming multiple layers, or adding bustle points, they handle every detail so your dress fits like it was custom-made. Most brides need 2-3 fittings to get everything just right.

Bridal Jewelry & Accessories

They carry a curated selection of bridal jewelry and accessories that won't break the bank but still photograph beautifully. Perfect for brides who want that finishing touch without the designer price tag.

General Sewing & Alterations

Beyond bridal, they handle everyday alterations for suits, dresses, and formal wear. It's the same attention to detail they bring to wedding gowns, just for your regular wardrobe needs.
