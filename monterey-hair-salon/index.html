Our Story

A corner of Monterey Blvd that never rushes you

Tucked between Edna and Foerster on Monterey Boulevard, this little salon sits in one of those San Francisco neighborhoods that still feels like a neighborhood. The kind where people walk to the coffee shop, know their mail carrier, and have had the same barber for years. That's exactly the energy inside Monterey Hair Salon — unhurried, personal, and genuinely focused on making you look good.

The people behind the chairs here take their craft seriously without taking themselves too seriously. They'll ask what you want, actually listen, and — this is rarer than it should be — tell you honestly if they think something else might suit you better. Whether you're coming in for a tight fade or a full cut and style, the attention is the same. No assembly line, no rush to get to the next client.

What keeps people coming back to a place like this isn't just the haircut — it's how the whole thing feels. You know your name is remembered. You know the person cutting your hair is paying attention. In a city that moves fast, having one place that slows down a little and just does good work on you is worth a lot. That's what 157 people have been trying to say.

"I've been going to Monterey Hair Salon for almost two years now and I genuinely can't imagine going anywhere else. I have curly, thick hair that most places butcher, and from my first visit they just... got it. Showed up yesterday after a rough week and walked out feeling like a completely different person. That sounds dramatic but it's just true."
