Our Story

The shop that Geary Boulevard built

Somewhere between Emerson and Wood Street, tucked into the everyday rhythm of Geary Boulevard, Great Cuts On Geary has become one of those places you just end up loyal to. It didn't happen overnight. It happened the second or third time you walked in, realized the wait wasn't bad, the price was fair, and you left looking genuinely good — not just passable.

The barbers here don't rush you. They take the time to actually look at your hair, ask what you want, and tell you honestly if something else might work better. That kind of straightforwardness is rarer than it sounds. In a city full of $60 haircut spots with booking apps and waitlists, this place just opens its doors at 10 and does the work — six days a week, no fuss.

People come back because it feels like their shop. Regulars from the neighborhood, folks who moved across the city but still make the drive out to Geary, guys bringing their sons in for the first time — they all find something here that's harder to name than a good haircut. It's the consistency. The familiarity. The fact that you walk out feeling like yourself, just a little cleaner.

What we do

Classic barbering, done right — no gimmicks, no hidden fees

Haircut

A proper sit-down haircut where someone actually listens before they pick up the scissors. Whether you come in with a reference photo or just say 'clean it up,' you'll leave looking like you meant to look that way.

Fade & Taper

The kind of fade that still looks good a week later — blended clean, edges tight, nothing harsh or choppy. The barbers here have done enough of these that they know exactly where the line should sit.

Beard Trim

A beard trim that actually shapes your face instead of just shortening your beard. They'll line it up, clean up the neck, and hand you a mirror when they're done — which is usually the moment you realize it was worth the trip.
