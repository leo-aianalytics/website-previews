Our Story

A corner spot that knows your name

TJ Cups landed on Noriega a few years back, right in the heart of the Inner Sunset where you've got families, students, and people just trying to get through their day. The neighborhood needed something real—not a chain, but a place with personality. Someone noticed that and built it here.

What started as a simple juice bar and bubble tea spot became something more by just paying attention. The drinks change with the seasons. The people behind the counter actually listen when you're ordering. They're not rushing you through; they're trying to nail what you want. Over time, 380 Yelp reviews later, people started coming back not just for the boba or the fresh-squeezed juice, but because it felt like a small thing done well.

On a foggy afternoon on Noriega, you'll see regulars dropping by after work, kids on the way home from school, someone on a laptop making this their afternoon spot. That's what keeps people coming back—it's not fancy, it's just honest. A really good drink at a fair price, made by people who show up the same time every day and care about what they're pouring.

What you'll find here

Drinks made fresh, flavors that change with the season, no pretense.

Bubble Tea

The kind that's made to order while you wait. Smooth tea bases, real boba, adjustable sweetness because people actually have different tastes. It's the drink that brings half the neighborhood through the door.

Fresh Juices & Smoothies

Bright, cold-pressed vibes without the wellness-industry sermon. Citrus, tropical, green drinks that taste like actual fruit. The kind of thing you grab when you need something real before a long afternoon.

Coffee & Tea

Simple coffee and tea done right—because not every visit is about bubble tea. Hot or iced, made with attention, the kind of reliable cup that becomes your go-to order over months of stopping in.
