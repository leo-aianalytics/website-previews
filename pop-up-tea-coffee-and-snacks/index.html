Our Story

Where bubble tea meets real coffee culture

Pop Up Tea started with a simple idea: why choose between a good coffee shop and a good boba place? Someone decided we shouldn't have to. On Branham Lane in San Jose, they built a spot that does both—and does them well. Over time, 716 reviews and countless regulars later, it's become clear that people were waiting for exactly this.

What makes it work is the attention to detail. The coffee isn't an afterthought next to the bubble tea menu, and the boba isn't just a novelty. Whether you're ordering a carefully pulled espresso or a custom tea with the right amount of sweetness and tapioca, you can tell someone who knows what they're doing is making it. The staff remembers orders. They ask how you like your drink. They care about getting it right.

People keep coming back because it feels like a real neighborhood spot, not a chain. You'll see regulars ordering their usual, students setting up laptops, friends meeting up for a catch-up over something cold. It's the kind of place where you can pop in at lunch, grab a juice, or come back at 9 p.m. on a Friday night and find exactly what you need. That consistency, that care—that's what builds a community.

What We Make

Fresh, customized drinks made to order, every time.

Bubble Tea & Specialty Teas

You pick the base, the sweetness level, and what goes in it. Whether you're a traditionalist or someone who wants to experiment, they'll make it exactly how you want it. This isn't assembly-line boba—it's made to your taste.

Coffee Done Right

Espresso drinks, pour-overs, lattes—made by people who actually know what they're doing. You can taste the difference between a drink made with care and one made in a rush. This is the former.

Fresh Juices & Smoothies

When you want something cold, fresh, and actually good for you. They make these from real fruit and ingredients, not concentrate. It's the kind of thing you actually feel good about drinking.
