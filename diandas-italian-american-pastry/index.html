Our Story

A sweet tradition between 24th and 25th

The Dianda family brought their pastry recipes from Italy to Mission Street decades ago, back when the neighborhood was different but the need for really good cannoli was exactly the same. What started as a small bakery serving the local Italian community has become a Mission District institution.

Three generations later, you'll still find family members behind the counter at 6 AM, rolling dough and filling cannoli shells the way their grandmother taught them. The display cases are packed with traditional Italian pastries alongside American favorites, because this neighborhood has always been about blending the old with the new.

Regular customers have been coming here for twenty, thirty years – some bringing their kids who are now bringing their own kids. It's the kind of place where they remember your usual order, where birthday cakes become family traditions, and where the smell of fresh baking draws you in from 24th Street.

What We Bake

Traditional Italian pastries and custom cakes made fresh daily

Italian Pastries

Croissants, cookies, and pastries baked fresh every morning starting at 6 AM. Perfect for your coffee run or when you need something sweet to brighten your day.
