Our Story

A corner of Ocean Avenue that still gets it right

On the stretch of Ocean Avenue between Ashton and Fairfield, not far from where the J-Church rattles past and the fog sits low in the morning, Ming Yuet Stylist has been doing what it does best — giving people a good haircut at a fair price without making a big deal of it. The shop sits in that sweet spot of the neighborhood where longtime residents grab their groceries, pick up lunch, and stop in for a trim without ever needing to think twice about it.

The people behind the chairs here have seen it all — the little kids who squirm, the regulars who come in every three weeks like clockwork, the folks who walked in once on a whim and never went back to their old place. There's a steadiness to the work here that you can feel the moment you sit down. No theatrics, no trend-chasing. Just clean lines, careful hands, and the kind of attention that makes you realize how rare it is to find someone who actually listens.

People come back to Ming Yuet because it never lets them down. Not in a flashy way — in the quiet, reliable way that actually means something when you're trying to look right for a job interview or a family photo or just another Monday. Over 170 reviews on Yelp, built one haircut at a time, tell you everything you need to know. This is the neighborhood barbershop that the neighborhood actually uses.

"I've been coming here for probably four years now and I still can't find a reason to go anywhere else. The price is unbeatable for San Francisco, and my cut always looks exactly the way I asked. It's cash-friendly, the wait is usually pretty reasonable if you come early, and honestly the vibe is just relaxed. No one's trying to sell you a $40 hair mask. Just a good haircut and you're on your way."

What We Do

Straightforward cuts and styling, done with care, priced so you don't wince at the register

Haircut

You sit down, you say what you want, and they do it — cleanly, quickly, and without making you feel like you needed a Pinterest board to explain yourself. The finish is sharp and the whole thing takes just long enough to feel like you were actually taken care of.

Women's Cut & Style

Whether you're after a trim to tidy things up or a proper restyle, the team here handles it without drama. It's the kind of salon visit that leaves you walking out onto Ocean Ave feeling like yourself, just a little more put-together.

Children's Haircut

Getting a kid through a haircut is its own kind of skill, and the stylists at Ming Yuet have plenty of it. Patient, calm, and fast enough to get it done before the squirming really kicks in.
